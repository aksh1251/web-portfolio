about me

I am Akshaya, a front-end web developer currently living in Berlin, Germany. I chose to dwell in the fine arts of frontend because I feel I have a knack for good design and what I'd like to call design discipline. I am a self-taught developer who loves to solve real world problems and make the world a better place, for humans and dogs, but mostly dogs .

Whatever team I work with, I bring honesty, loyalty and out-of-the-box thinking to the table. I am a quick learner and even quicker when working with people who are passionate about what they do. To know more about me, reach out to me on aksh1997ak@gmail.com or using the contact form.
